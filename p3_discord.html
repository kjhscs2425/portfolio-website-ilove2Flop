Invite my discord bot

You can talk to the bot by sending it messages like:

  • hello – The bot will greet you personally.
  • bot – It will let you know it's alive and listening.
  • help – Get a quick overview of what it can do.
  • joke – Want a laugh? Ask it for a joke!
  • how are you – The bot will tell you how it's doing.
  • weather – While it can't check the real forecast, it has something fun to say.
  • flip – Ask it to flip a coin and see if you get heads or tails.
  • random – The bot will give you a random number between 1 and 100.
  • math – Get a silly math fact.
  • fact – Learn something random and interesting!
